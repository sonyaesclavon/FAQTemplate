Frequently asked questions

Are your freelancers first time learners or experienced programmers?

We have a mix of both on our site but all our freelancers have at least worked on their own coding project before so they have the practical experience needed to complete freelance tasks.

What is the difference between task applications and task submissions?

The task applications tab show the profiles of the users who have applied for your task. The task submissions tab shows the final submission and all the attached files from the user you have hired.
